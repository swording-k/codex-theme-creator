# GT Static Wallpaper V2 Design

## Goal

Create a photorealistic private GT racing wallpaper designed specifically for the unmodified Dream Skin static theme contract, then validate it in the real Codex home and task routes.

## Composition

- Opaque `2560 x 1440` landscape wallpaper with no UI, text, HUD, watermark, or generated interface elements.
- The left `0%..55%` remains dark, calm, and low-detail for Codex navigation, title, and suggestion labels.
- The main Porsche 911 GT3 RS sits at `62%..88%` in a medium-distance rear three-quarter view.
- The Porsche uses a continuous rear light bar, believable license-plate recess, rear bumper, diffuser, wing, and wheel geometry.
- A BMW M4 GT3 and Mercedes-AMG GT3 appear farther down the pit lane or track and remain recognizable without competing with the Porsche.
- The bottom-center composer zone uses restrained wet asphalt with soft reflections and no bright clutter.

## Visual Direction

Premium commercial motorsport photography, realistic materials and proportions, cool pit lighting balanced by restrained warm garage light, visible track depth, no heavy fog, and no horror-like darkness.

## Runtime

The private Porsche theme returns to the static Dream Skin contract. The experimental motion profile is removed. The existing dark palette remains unless the rendered background requires a small contrast correction during real Codex validation.

## Acceptance

- The background passes Dream Skin payload validation at `2560 x 1440`.
- Suggestion card labels remain readable on the home route.
- The car and track remain visible around existing-chat content.
- No animated white rain lines are present.
